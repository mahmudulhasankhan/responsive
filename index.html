In the history mankind around the world has many days to remember. International Mother Language Day is one of the memorable days. Every independent nation observes the day in memory of the language martyrs and speaks in praise of the mother tongue. 21 February of every year is regarded as the International Mother Language Day.On 21 February 1952, during the tome of Pakistani rule some valiant sons of our country laid down their lives for our mother tongue, bangle. Since then, the day had been observed as our shaheed day OUN as the international mother language day.
